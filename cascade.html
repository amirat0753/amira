Government

types of government

legislative government;The legislative branch is responsible for creating laws.


Judiciary is the third arm of government, whose primary role is to interpret laws enacted by the Legislature and applies such existing law to individual cases, in other to settle disputes between two private citizens or between private citizen(s) and government


The executive is the organ of government responsible for the implementation, execution or enforcement of laws and policies made by the legislative arm of the government.

government is an exercise of political authority over the actions, affairs, etc, of a political unit, people, etc, as well as the performance of certain functions for this unit or body; the action of governing.
